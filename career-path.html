IB Career-related Programme (CP)

The IB Career-related Programme (CP) is designed for students aged 16-19 who wish to engage in career-related learning while gaining transferable and lifelong skills.

Key Features

  • Career-related study tailored to student interests
  • Integration of IB courses with practical skills
  • Core components including Personal and Professional Skills, Service Learning, and Reflective Project
